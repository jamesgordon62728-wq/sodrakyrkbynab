Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data

Last updated: 2024. This policy applies to Södra Kyrkbyn AB.

1. Data Controller

Södra Kyrkbyn AB, Gamla Lundbygatan 17, 418 75 Göteborg, Sweden, is the data controller responsible for your personal data processed in connection with our services.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number
  • Business information provided during service inquiries
  • Communication records when you contact us

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds under GDPR: (a) to perform a contract with you or take pre-contractual steps; (b) to comply with legal obligations; (c) based on our legitimate interests in operating our business.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to: respond to your inquiries; provide our services; maintain business records; and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Business records are generally kept for 7 years in accordance with Swedish accounting law (Bokföringslagen).

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to: access your personal data; rectify inaccurate data; request erasure ('right to be forgotten'); object to processing; restrict processing; and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sodrakyrkbyn.se.

7. Third Parties & Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared only where required by law or with processors acting on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements.

8. Cookies

This website uses only essential local storage to remember your language preference. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

9. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related questions or to lodge a complaint, please contact:

Södra Kyrkbyn AB
Gamla Lundbygatan 17, 418 75 Göteborg, Sweden
info@sodrakyrkbyn.se

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY): www.imy.se
