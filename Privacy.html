Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data

Last updated: 2026. Applies to website and mobile apps.

Website Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Södra Kyrkbyn AB collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

Information we collect

When you submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), and message content. This information is stored securely and used solely to respond to your inquiry.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, provide requested services, and communicate with you about our consulting services. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please use the contact form on our Contact Us page.

Mobile Apps Privacy Policy

Our mobile applications are designed with privacy as a core principle.

No personal data collection

Our mobile apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

Local processing only

All app functionality operates entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device and is not synchronized with any external servers.

No tracking or analytics

We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or any third-party monitoring tools in our mobile applications.

Offline functionality

Our apps are designed to work completely offline, ensuring your data remains private and under your control at all times.
