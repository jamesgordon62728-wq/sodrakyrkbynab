Who We Are

A Trusted Business in the Heart of Göteborg

Södra Kyrkbyn AB is a Swedish limited company (Aktiebolag) headquartered in Göteborg, one of Sweden's largest cities and a major hub for business and innovation.

Our company is built on principles of transparency, legal compliance, and professional service delivery. We operate in full accordance with Swedish corporate law and uphold the highest standards of business ethics.

Registered at Gamla Lundbygatan 17 in the Göteborg area, we serve our clients with dedication and professionalism. Our location in Göteborg provides excellent connectivity to the broader Swedish and Scandinavian business landscape.
